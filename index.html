OUR

STORY

Somewhere between quick glances, two strangers met 12 years ago. Something had already woven in their hearts — quiet, unnoticed, waiting. A love that lasted only a moment in a small cliché, that disappeared into time, but some things don't fade. Because love like theirs waits. It began on the first day of a work orientation, where two lives briefly met without knowing what was to come. She didn’t notice him then— but he remembered her enough to find her name and her number on a log sheet passed around the room, and from that small, unexpected gesture, a connection began. From that small beginning grew a story filled with distance, pauses, and unexpected turns. Over the years, their story was not without pauses; there were moments when life pulled them apart. Yet somehow, through time and distance, they found their way back to each other. And now, after 12 years of beginnings, endings, and finding love again—they choose forever.
